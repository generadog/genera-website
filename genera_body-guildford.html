Getting Started

How it works.

01
Get in touch

Fill in our enquiry form or give us a call. Tell us about your dog — breed, age, temperament — and we'll take it from there.

02
Meet & greet

We arrange a complimentary visit to our Cobham facility so your dog can see the space and our team can meet them properly.

03
Assessment day

Every dog does a settling-in session before joining the group. We take it at their pace — no rushing, no pressure.

04
First day in the woodland

Once everyone's happy, your dog joins us. Drop them at our facility each morning and we'll care for them all day — home happy and tired by the end of the day.
